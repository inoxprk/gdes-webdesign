My Passion

Hmmm... What is my passion? This one was difficult was because my passions and interests always seem to change and be all over the place. Here are some of my passions:

  • Sushi

    I love sushi with passion. Although I wish I had all the money to fund this particular passion of mine, but I do like sushi a lot. I like how there are different sushi by region and season, and the sense of craftsmanship that goes into making sushi. It's very simple yet complicated food.

  • UX/UI

    What I like about UX/UI is that it's a field about designing for someone in mind. I research, interview, and empathize with the potential end users to really understand what they need. This gives a reason for and a value to my design. You know when you are cooking food for someone and you see them enjoying your food? It's something like that I like about UX/UI.

  • Books

    When I ask my friends about their favorite books, most of the times they tell me some form of novel books. Science fiction, Mystery, Crime, Romance, etc. However, I.. have somewhat unusual(?) preference. I like reading books about certain field of study. For example, I really liked a textbook I had to read for my mass communications class. Yes - The exam and memorization part was defintely not my favorite. But I do enjoy the learning part.
